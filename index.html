What is this app for?

This app connects to your Google Calendar and retrieves the date and time of your next appointments then calculates how much time is left until the one closest in time and displays a countdown to that date on the screen.

What this app will do with your data?

Calendar information is downloaded locally to your phone directly from Google to calculate when your next appointment starts. This app does not retain or upload any information to the cloud.
